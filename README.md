# Coffee_Sales_PowerBI (Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI)
## Project Objective
An interactive Coffee Sales Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, product contribution, and customer purchasing behavior across different time segments. The dashboard integrates multiple KPIs, applies data transformation processes, and delivers dynamic business insights through slicer-driven interactivity.

## Dataset
- <a href="https://github.com/fahamidamahi/Coffee_Project/blob/master/Coffe_sales.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Problem Statement
- KPIs at the top: Total Sales Revenue, Total Transactions, and Average Price per Transaction.
- Trend visuals: Monthly sales trends tracking both revenue amount and transaction volume across all 12 months.
- Time-of-day insights: Revenue distribution across Morning, Afternoon, and Night segments.
- Product insights: Revenue generated by each coffee product and top products driving overall sales.
- Interactivity: Ability to filter by product type, time of day, and month to explore granular insights.
- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/fahamidamahi/Coffee_Project/blob/master/final%20dashboard.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
1️⃣ Data Import & Transformation
- Imported raw sales datasets into Power BI
- Cleaned and structured data using Power Query
- Handled inconsistencies and formatting issues
- Transformed date and time fields for time-based analysis
- Created new calculated columns for time-of-day segmentation

2️⃣ Data Modeling
- Established relationships between:
- Sales ↔ Product (via Product ID)
- Sales ↔ Date Table (via Date)
- Built a structured star-schema data model inside Power BI

3️⃣ Analysis Layer
- Created DAX measures for key metrics:
- Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Amount])
- Total Transactions = COUNTROWS(Sales)
- Average Price = DIVIDE([Total Sales], [Total Transactions])
- Designed KPI card summaries

4️⃣ Visualization & Dashboard
- Built interactive charts and visuals
- Designed a dark-themed dynamic dashboard layout
- Implemented Slicers for filtering:
          Month,
          Product Category,
          Time of Day,
- Developed a fully interactive reporting interface

## Business Insights Derived
- March recorded the highest sales volume, indicating a strong seasonal or promotional spike.
- Sales are nearly equally distributed across Morning, Afternoon, and Night — reflecting all-day customer demand.
- Latte is the top-selling product by a significant margin, followed by Americano with Milk and Cappuccino.
- Espresso has the lowest sales volume despite being a core coffee product — a potential opportunity for targeted promotion.
- Transaction volume and revenue amount follow similar monthly trends, suggesting consistent average price throughout the year.

## Conclusion
The dashboard delivers a clear view of coffee sales performance, highlighting product popularity, time-based purchasing behavior, and monthly revenue trends. It enables quick identification of top-performing products and peak sales periods, supporting effective data-driven business decisions.
